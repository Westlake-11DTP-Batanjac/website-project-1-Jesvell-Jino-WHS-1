Clouds can weigh far more than most people expect. A large fluffy cloud might look light and soft, but it can contain thousands of kilograms of water droplets spread throughout the air. It stays floating because the droplets are spread out over a huge area and are supported by rising warm air currents.

Octopuses are strange and fascinating creatures because they have three hearts and blue blood. Two hearts help pump blood to the gills, while the third sends blood around the rest of the body. They are also extremely intelligent and can solve puzzles, open containers, and even escape from tanks.

The smell of rain on dry ground has a special name: petrichor. It happens when rain hits soil and tiny compounds from plants and bacteria are released into the air. Many people enjoy the smell because humans can detect it surprisingly well, especially after a long dry period.
