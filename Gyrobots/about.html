About the company!

At Gyrobots, we are passionate about pushing the boundaries of robotics and artificial intelligence to create innovative machines that make life easier, safer, and more exciting. By combining advanced robotics, computer vision, and deep learning technologies, we develop intelligent robots capable of seeing, learning, adapting, and interacting with the world around them. Inspired by the creativity of Tim Sweeney, the agility and resilience of Muhammad Ali, and the visionary engineering of Nikola Tesla, our designs blend imagination, performance, and cutting-edge technology. Our flagship model, the AMIHR, showcases this philosophy through its versatile capabilities, uniquely engineered arms, and futuristic design, delivering exceptional precision, strength, and adaptability across a wide range of tasks. At Gyrobots, we are committed to shaping the future of AI-powered robotics and inspiring the next generation of technological possibilities.
