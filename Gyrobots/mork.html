ROBOTICS

MODEL KOLA

Tim Sweeney, Muhammad Ali, and Nikola Tesla each inspired key parts of this robot’s design. Tim Sweeney influenced the creative and interactive elements, pushing the idea of a futuristic, user-focused system similar to advanced game worlds. Muhammad Ali inspired the robot’s defensive movements and agility, shaping how it reacts quickly and protects itself in dynamic situations. Nikola Tesla influenced the energy systems, inspiring efficient and powerful electrical design concepts that drive the robot’s performance.

MODEL AMIHR

The AMIHR is Gyrobots’ newest and most versatile robotic model, designed to handle a wide range of tasks with precision and adaptability. Its uniquely engineered arms are built for both strength and flexibility, making them ideal for detailed work as well as heavy-duty tasks. While its design may not look entirely human, its unconventional body structure allows for greater movement, balance, and efficiency in different environments. The AMIHR stands out as a powerful example of how innovation and functionality can come together in a bold, futuristic design.
